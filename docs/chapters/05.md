### 🧠 Core Game Logic (Blueprint: `WiseKnightsTrialLogic.cs`)
- **Mechanic:** Brain Reflex & Knowledge Scaling.
- **Logic:**
    - **Question Timer:** 15 seconds per question.
    - **Scoring:** Base 40 + `timeLeft * 2` + `streakBonus`.
    - **Star Threshold:** 400+ points for a "Diamond Star" (3 Stars).
- **Boss Phase:** Final interaction involves "sealing" the Lord of Decay with 10 correct knowledge answers.

---

### 🔄 Data & Sync Flow
1. **Achievement:** Unlocks the `CHAMPION_OF_ENAMEL` achievement in the backend.
2. **XP:** Large XP dump (150-200) often triggers multiple Level Ups.
3. **Sync:** Final results are synchronized with both Firebase Auth and the local SQLite database.
