# 🍗 Chapter 04: Healthy Eating Habits

### 🌟 Technical Overview
- **Title:** Healthy Eating Habits
- **Status:** Integrated & Educational
- **Game Type:** `food-sort-fantasy`
- **Primary Component:** `src/components/games/KingsBanquet.tsx`

---

### 🎨 Frontend Details
- **UI Logic:** Drag-and-drop or Swipe-based sorting of food items.
- **Visuals:** Uses custom food assets from `src/assets/food/` (Apple, Broccoli, Lollipop, etc.).
- **Interactive Feedback:** Animates a "Sparkle" for correct answers and a "Shake" for incorrect ones.

---

### 🧠 Core Game Logic (Blueprint: `KingsBanquetLogic.cs`)
- **Mechanic:** Categorical Sorting.
- **Data:** A database of 15+ food items with a `bool isHealthy` flag.
- **Logic:**
    - **Streak Bonus:** Every correct answer adds to a streak; bonus points are given for streaks of 3, 5, and 10.
    - **Scoring:** `25 + Mathf.Min(streak * 5, 20)`.
- **Educational Goal:** Teach players that even "unseen" sugars in soda are bad for enamel.

---

### 🔄 Data & Sync Flow
1. **Result:** Transfers `score` and `stars` to `handleGameComplete` in `ChaptersScreen.tsx`.
2. **Gold:** High gold reward (20-50) is awarded to encourage "purchasing" items in the shop.
3. **Backend:** SQLite `users` table tracks the highest `chapter_4_score` achieved.
