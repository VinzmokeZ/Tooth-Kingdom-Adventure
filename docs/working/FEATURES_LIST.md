# Tooth Kingdom Adventure - Feature Inventory

### 🛡️ 1. Security & Identity
- **Multi-Factor Auth**: Support for Phone OTP and Biometric login (Face/Touch ID).
- **Hero Profiles**: Customizable user profiles with hero name and avatar selection.
- **Social Login**: Google, Apple, and Facebook SSO integration.

### 🎮 2. Gameplay Mechanics
- **Chapter System**: 6 unique adventures mapped to dental health tasks.
- **RPG Rewards**: Earn XP and Gold for every successful mission.
- **Game Engine**: A flexible switchboard to load React, Unity (Chapter 1), Godot (Chapter 2), or GameMaker (Chapter 6) games.

### 📚 3. Education Layer
- **Learning Academy**: AI-powered courses on brushing and nutrition.
- **Daily Tips**: Real-time advice from the Chibi Guide (powered by Gemini AI).
- **Resources**: In-app PDF guides and video tutorials for children.

### 📈 4. Progression & Tracking
- **Streak Tracker**: Encourages 7, 14, and 30-day brushing habits.
- **Health System**: Enamel health decreases if tasks are missed and increases with successful brushes.
- **Activity Calendar**: Persistent visual history of all dental activity.
- **Achievements**: Unlockable badges for mastering specific dental skills.

### 🎨 5. User Interface
- **Mobile-First Design**: Optimized for touch interaction and phone screens.
- **Adaptive Theme**: Full Light and Dark mode support.
- **Chibi Guide**: Animated characters that provide guidance throughout the app experience.
