# games_detailed_01_06.md: Chapter Master List

### Chapter 01: Cavity Miner
- **Logic**: `CavityMinerLogic.cs`.
- **Mechanic**: Grid-based exploration. Clear cavities before the move limit.
- **Rewards**: 75 XP, 1-3 Stars.

### Chapter 02: Shark Dentist
- **Logic**: `SharkDentistLogic.gd`.
- **Mechanic**: Precision extraction. Clean the Rotting Teeth before the timer hits 0.
- **Rewards**: Gold bonus for 100% Shark Health.

### Chapter 03: Flossing Rescue
- **Logic**: `RoyalRopeRescueLogic.cs`.
- **Mechanic**: Physics-based flossing. Swing between obstacles to save the kingdom.
- **Rewards**: High XP for center-line precision.

### Chapter 04: Healthy Eating
- **Logic**: `KingsBanquetLogic.cs`.
- **Mechanic**: Sorting game. Food items are Categorized as "Healthy" or "Sugary".
- **Rewards**: +50 Gold for perfect streaks.

### Chapter 05: Master Trial
- **Logic**: `WiseKnightsTrialLogic.cs`.
- **Mechanic**: Knowledge Trivia + Boss Defense. Prove your dental mastery.
- **Rewards**: Unlocks the "Master Crown" badge.

### Chapter 06: Battle Dentist
- **Logic**: `BattleDentistLogic.gml`.
- **Mechanic**: Retro Top-Down Combat. Blast the Grime Monsters away with Cleaning Blasts.
- **Rewards**: 12 Stars final completion.
